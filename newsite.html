🚨BREAKING NEWS: AI-Powered Pizza Drone Delivers First Order in NYC!🍕

Written by Gary Watts

New York City, April 29, 2025 — The future of fast food has arrived, and it’s flying. In a headline-grabbing first, NYC witnessed the successful delivery of a hot pepperoni pizza by drone this morning — no traffic, no tipping, no delay. The autonomous craft, powered by AI and backed by food tech startup SkySlice, touched down in Central Park just 9 minutes after the order was placed. “We’re not just slicing pizza — we’re slicing delivery time,” said SkySlice CEO Jenna Lin. “This is just the beginning.” The drone, nicknamed "Pie-Oneer," uses real-time traffic data, weather sensors, and a sleek thermal box to keep the pizza piping hot mid-air. It dropped the pie to a designated landing pad where a customer scanned a code to retrieve it. Mayor Eric Adams called the test “a taste of tomorrow” and hinted at a citywide rollout later this year. Critics raised questions about air traffic safety, privacy, and whether drones will replace human jobs. But for now, one thing’s clear: the race for airborne takeout has officially taken off.
